The output is found by first taking the intersection of results between filtering options and the union of results within filtering options.

Then, those tracks have the popularity and duration constraints placed on them.

i.e. "Alice" and "Bob" and "Hold the Girl" with popularity 50 to 20 shows all songs in the database by (Alice OR Bob) AND on "Hold the Girl" with 50 >= popularity >= 20

Filtering options are all taken from your existing playlists or new Recommendations playlist.
